## Description

This game introduces two worlds in one. The player can move through the red world at a faster and more chaotic pace then when crossing over to the yellow world where moves at a slower and a calmer pace. The game implements emotions into how the player interacts with the two different worlds.

---

## Setup and Interaction Instructions

Use the WASD keys or the arrow keys to move the player around and explore the world.

---

## Iteration Notes

Post-playtest: three changes made based on playtesting 1. Made the rectangles appear all over the map at random 2. Changed the colour of the player to match the colours in both worlds 3. Changed the speed of the yellow world to make it slower
